Voxel Racing [workTitle]

Racing game

A racing-RPG inspired by 'Mario Kart Tour' and 'Super Mario Kart'.
I made this small demo in Unity as a start for a complete RPG racing Game, i am still currently developing this game for a complete gameplay demo.

Sprites

I start off with a fairly simple sprite sheet with 22 racer sprites, 3 emotes, lose/neutral/win and a row of celebration poses that differ per racer.


I made this character sheet myself and although it was difficult i'm very happy with .

Models

I made the kart models in 'MagicaVoxel' which made the sprite based characters pop even more. I didn't want flat karts together with the character because i feel like 3D karts add extra dept and visual appeal to a flat sprite-based character
